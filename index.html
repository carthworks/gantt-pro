Getting Started

1
Create Your First Project

Click the "Add Project" button in the sidebar to create a new project. Fill in the project name, start date, duration, and other details.

2
Add Tasks to Projects

Click the "+" icon next to any project to add tasks. Tasks help you break down projects into manageable pieces.

3
Track Progress

Update task progress using the slider when editing. The Gantt chart will visually show your progress with colored bars.

Key Features

Search & Filter

Use the search bar to find projects and tasks. Apply filters to view specific progress ranges or statuses.

Progress Velocity

Track your team's completion rate and get estimated completion dates based on current velocity.

Smart Suggestions

Get AI-powered recommendations on what to work on today based on deadlines, dependencies, and priorities.

Export & Share

Export your data as JSON or CSV. Share your Gantt chart with team members using shareable links.

Tips & Tricks

Dependencies: Set task dependencies to automatically visualize project flow and identify blocking tasks.

View Modes: Switch between Day, Week, Month, and Year views to see different time scales of your project.

Color Coding: Use different colors for projects to visually organize and categorize your work.

Regular Updates: Keep your progress updated regularly to get accurate velocity tracking and completion estimates.
