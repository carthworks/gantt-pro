## How to Use

1. **Click the extension icon** to open Gantt Pro in a new tab
2. **Add projects** using the "Add Project" button
3. **Add tasks** to projects by expanding them
4. **Track progress** with visual progress bars
5. **Use smart suggestions** to prioritize your work
6. **Monitor velocity** to predict completion dates
7. **Export data** for backups or sharing

## Privacy & Security
