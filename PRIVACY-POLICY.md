## Overview

Gantt Pro ("the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information.

## Data Collection

**We do NOT collect any personal data.**

The Extension operates entirely locally in your browser and does not:
- Collect any personal information
- Track your usage
- Send data to external servers
- Use analytics or tracking tools
- Require user accounts or authentication

## Data Storage

All project and task data you create is stored **locally** in your browser using Chrome's storage API. This data:
- Remains on your device only
- Is never transmitted to external servers
- Is accessible only by the Extension
- Can be deleted by uninstalling the Extension or clearing browser data

## Data You Control

You have complete control over your data:
- **Export**: Download your data as JSON or CSV files
- **Import**: Restore data from JSON backups
- **Delete**: Remove all data by uninstalling the Extension

## Permissions

The Extension requests the following permissions:

### storage
- **Purpose**: To save your projects and tasks locally in your browser
- **Data Stored**: Project names, task details, dates, progress, colors, and descriptions
- **Access**: Only the Extension can access this data

### tabs
- **Purpose**: To open the Extension in a new fullscreen tab for better user experience
- **Data Accessed**: None - only used to create a new tab

## Third-Party Services

The Extension does NOT use any third-party services, including:
- No analytics (e.g., Google Analytics)
- No advertising networks
- No social media integrations
- No external APIs or servers

## Data Security

Your data is protected by:
- Chrome's built-in security for local storage
- No network transmission of your data
- No external access to your information

## Children's Privacy

The Extension does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The Extension is designed for general project management use and does not target children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above. Continued use of the Extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

## Data Deletion

To delete all data stored by the Extension:

1. **Option 1**: Uninstall the Extension
   - Right-click the Extension icon
   - Select "Remove from Chrome"
   - All data will be automatically deleted

2. **Option 2**: Clear Extension data
   - Go to `chrome://settings/content/all`
   - Find "Gantt Pro"
   - Click "Clear data"
